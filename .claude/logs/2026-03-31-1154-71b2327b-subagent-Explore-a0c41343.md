### 1. **Hit Direction Formula Silent Failure When Ball Is At Player Center**

### 2. **Touch Coordinate Transformation Ignores HiDPI Canvas Scaling**
**Phase:** Phase 5 — AI Opponent & Mobile Controls

**What fails:** The interpolation formula is:

using `lerp = a + (b-a) * Math.max(0, Math.min(1, t))`. **Fix:** Track client/server clock offset and adjust `renderTimestamp` adaptively, or detect when `renderTimestamp` is consistently out of bounds and resync the interpolation buffer.
